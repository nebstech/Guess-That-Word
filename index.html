How to Play

  • A word will be displayed with its letters shuffled.
  • Use the input field to enter your guess.
  • Click the "Enter Word" button or press Enter on your keyboard to submit your guess.
  • If your guess is correct, you'll move to the next word.
  • If your guess is incorrect, you'll receive feedback and can try again.
  • You have 25 seconds to guess each word. The timer starts when you begin the game.
